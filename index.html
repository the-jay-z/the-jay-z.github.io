Research tool. This score estimates group-level risk of discharge to a destination other than home (e.g., skilled nursing or rehabilitation facility). It supports preoperative counseling and early discharge planning — it does not replace clinical judgment or determine care for an individual patient. Total score ranges 0–39; risk categories and observed rates are from the study test set (validation AUC 0.78).

Selvasingh JA, Jella TK, Krueger MA, Menon TP, Magdycz WP, Shinn JR. Who Goes Home? Predicting Non-Home Discharge After Neck Dissection. Department of Surgery, Section of Otolaryngology, Carilion Clinic – Virginia Tech Carilion School of Medicine.
